# Supplementary Materials Guide

`figures/final_manuscript_pdf/` contains the final figure assets used in the manuscript.

## Fit-Readiness and Stage-II Tables

- `results/fit_readiness/` contains aggregate fit-readiness summaries.
- `results/stage2/` contains aggregate Stage-II summaries and transport diagnostics.
- `results/stage2_support/` and `figures/stage2_support/` contain supporting Stage-II results and figures.

## Calibration Support

`results/calibration_support/` and `figures/calibration_support/` contain the supporting tables and five figures for the Stage-II calibration discussion:

- PIT calibration shape.
- Fixed-threshold outlier-screening behavior.
- Tail-family comparison.
- Top-tail profile across the sampled phase space.
- Regime-dependent scale and coverage behavior.

These materials support the manuscript's bounded conclusions: pull-width and distributional-shape effects dominate the tested stress behavior, top pull tails are not confined to one geometric extreme, and no universal scalar rescaling is supported.
